Perkins Bridge Initiative

FY25 Analysis Report

Program Objective

The Perkins Bridge Initiative provides a self-paced online Math Basic Skills Bridge designed to be taken concurrently with gateway mathematics courses (MAT115, MAT120, MAT200).

Process: Students take a diagnostic assessment during week 1. Those scoring below 7.0 mastery threshold receive Bridge access and tutoring support (in-person & Zoom).

Goal: Ensure smoother transitions and improve academic outcomes for students with foundational skill gaps.
